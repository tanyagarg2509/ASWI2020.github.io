About ACM@IGDTUW

ACM Student Chapter at IGDTUW is the technical society at Department of Information Technology, IGDTUW. It is student driven society where student active members organize plethora of events during the semester. For more details about the chapter, refer: http://acmigdtuwchapter.tech/

While most events are organized by students, sometimes particularly during summer/winter breaks, faculty driven workshops are organized, and this summer workshop 2020 is one such event.

Purpose: To augment conventional curriculum based learning by exposition of essential technical topics keeping an emphasis on project development with "learning by doing" approach.
